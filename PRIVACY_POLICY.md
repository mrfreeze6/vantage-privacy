# Privacy Policy for Vantage

**Last updated: April 22, 2026**

Vantage ("the app") is a journaling application. This policy explains what the app does and does not collect, store, and share. The app is operated by Bryson (the "developer").

## The short version

Your journal entries — including text, voice notes, photos, mood selections, and any other content you create — are stored only on your device. The developer does not have access to them. No journal content is transmitted off your device by the app.

## What the app stores on your device

The app stores the following information locally on your device, in a private database that only the app can read:

- The text of journal entries you write
- Voice recordings you create within the app
- Photos you attach to entries
- Mood selections you make
- Your settings (such as your chosen daily reminder time)
- Your progress through any guided programs you start

This information stays on your device. If you uninstall the app, this information is removed along with it. The app does not back up your entries to any cloud service unless and until you explicitly enable a cloud-backup feature in a future version (which is not currently active).

## What the app does not do

- The app does not collect analytics about how you use it.
- The app does not contain advertising or advertising trackers.
- The app does not require an account, email address, or login.
- The app does not transmit your journal entries, voice notes, photos, or mood data to the developer or any third party.
- The app does not sell, rent, or share any personal information, because it does not collect any.

## Permissions the app requests

The app may ask for the following permissions on your device. Each is used only for the purpose described, and only when you actively use the related feature:

- **Microphone**: Used only when you tap the record button to create a voice note. Recording stops when you tap stop. The app does not listen to or record audio at any other time.
- **Camera**: Used only when you tap to attach a photo to an entry. The app does not capture images at any other time.
- **Photos / Storage**: Used only when you tap to attach an existing photo from your device to an entry.
- **Notifications**: Used only to deliver the daily journaling reminder, if you choose to enable one in Settings. You can disable this at any time.

## Children's privacy

The app is not directed at children under 13. The developer does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. Because the app does not collect any information from any user, this is in practice the case for all ages.

## Changes to this policy

If this policy changes — for example, if a future version of the app introduces optional cloud backup — the updated policy will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. The app will not begin transmitting any journal data off your device without your explicit opt-in.
